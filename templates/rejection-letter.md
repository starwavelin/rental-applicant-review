# Rejection Letter Template (Adverse Action Notice)

---

[Date]

[Applicant Full Name]
[Applicant Address]

**Re: Rental Application — [Property Address]**

Dear [Applicant Name],

Thank you for your interest in renting our apartment at [Property Address],
and for taking the time to submit your application and supporting documents.

After careful review of your application, we regret to inform you that we
are unable to approve your application at this time.

**Reason for Denial:**
☐ Insufficient income (does not meet our minimum income requirement of 40x monthly rent)
☐ Credit history does not meet our minimum requirements
☐ Negative rental history / landlord reference
☐ Background check results
☐ Other: _______________

**Credit Reporting Agency (if credit was a factor):**
If this decision was based in whole or in part on information from a
consumer reporting agency, you have the right to obtain a free copy of
your report within 60 days by contacting:

[Credit Bureau Name, e.g. Equifax]
[Address / Phone / Website]

You also have the right to dispute the accuracy of information in your report
directly with the reporting agency.

We wish you the best in your housing search.

Sincerely,

_______________
[Landlord Name]
[Phone / Email]

---

*Note to landlord: Keep a copy of this letter for your records.
Per NYC law, you must provide a written reason for denial.
Do NOT mention age, children, national origin, religion, disability,
or source of income (Section 8) as reasons for denial.*
